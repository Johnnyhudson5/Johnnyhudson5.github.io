Hasselback Garlic Bread Grilled Cheese

Prep Time: 25 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 35 minutes

Yields: 6

Think garlic bread, but way more fun. This hasselback grilled cheese has more surface area for that garlicky butter to soak in and get extra crispy, with melty cheese hiding in the iddle. Rip off little squares as you eat and enjoy a cheese pull with every bite.

Hasselback Garlic Bread Grilled Cheese

This isn’t your basic grilled cheese. We’re taking it up a notch with garlic butter, crispy golden edges, and melty cheese in every bite. But the best part? The top slice gets the Hasselback treatment, which means more square footage for crispiness and cheesy goodness. Each little cut toasts up perfectly so you can rip off crispy, buttery squares as you go — it’s grilled cheese like you’ve never had before.
